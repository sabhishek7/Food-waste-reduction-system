About Food Waste

Food waste is a significant global issue with environmental, economic, and social impacts. Each year, millions of tons of food are wasted, contributing to greenhouse gas emissions and resource depletion. Reducing food waste can help alleviate hunger, save resources, and mitigate environmental damage.

In recent times, the volume of food wasted has increased due to various factors such as supply chain inefficiencies, overproduction, and consumer behavior. Efforts to tackle food waste include improving food distribution, enhancing food recovery programs, and educating the public on sustainable practices.
